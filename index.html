What is Future Bus.


Is an application that makes the visually impaired Get on the right buses and increase the convenience of staff in providing services to the visually impaired





Our Feature.


  • It makes it easier for bus operators to help the visually impaired.


  • Help make the journey of the visually impaired more convenient.


  • Able to show travel information for the visually impaired


  • can show travel time and approximate distance
